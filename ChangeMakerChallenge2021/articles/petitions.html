Petitions

Petitions are an amazing way to gather a support base of people who care about an issue. It expresses the voice of the people and shows both the public and decision-makers the level of public support for an issue. Luckily there’s no shortage of environmental problems that the earth needs us to stand up for. Whether you want to sign petitions about putting an end to drilling, banning Target bags, encouraging Biden’s administration to continue their fight against climate change, or ending environmentally harmful activities such as trophy hunting, chances are you can find a petition for it! It only takes a few minutes and really does make a difference.

Here are a few websites where you browse through possible petitions!

  • https://www.change.org/t/climate-change-3

  • https://www.edf.org/action
