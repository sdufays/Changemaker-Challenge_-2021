Concerned about the Climate

As the world warms, sea levels rise, storms and extreme weather events become more frequent, sometimes it can be hard to imagine what we as individuals can do to truly make a difference.

So what can we do to stop this?

We may think that planetary destruction is something out of our control, but the earth has been facing a human-imposed climate catastrophe since the industrial revolution. Nowadays, each one of our actions make a difference, but it is only together we can create an everlasting change.

So what can we do to spark that change?

Do you recycle? Well chances are, you’re not doing it right.

In Orange County, the recycling rejection rate was 85% in 2020. In Fort Lauderdale, around twenty-five percent of what comes in isn’t recyclable. And in 2018, Clearwater sent ⅓ of their recyclable materials to be incinerated because they were contaminated.

Check out our recycling guidelines article to find out how to recycle correctly in South Florida!
